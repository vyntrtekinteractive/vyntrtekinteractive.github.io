Privacy Policy

Vyntrtek Interactive develops hypercasual games for Facebook Instant Games.

We do not directly collect or store personally identifiable information such as names, emails, or contact details.

Gameplay Data Collection

To improve gameplay experience and performance, we collect limited non-personal gameplay data through secure HTTPS events. This may include:

  • Game start events
  • Session start time
  • Level entry events
  • Level completion or failure events

This data may be processed using services such as Firebase and is used only for gameplay improvement, balancing, and performance analysis.

Third-Party Services

We use Meta Audience Network for advertising (rewarded and interstitial ads).

These services may collect:

  • Device information
  • Gameplay interactions
  • Advertising identifiers

All such data is handled by Meta under their policies.

Facebook Platform

As a Facebook Instant Game, we may access and use data provided by Facebook in accordance with Facebook’s Data Policy.

Data Usage

The data we collect is used solely to improve gameplay experience, analyze player progression, and monitor game performance. We do not sell or share user data beyond the services listed above.

Data Retention

We retain non-personal gameplay data only for as long as necessary to analyze performance and improve gameplay. Data may be periodically deleted or anonymized.

Children’s Privacy

Our games are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
